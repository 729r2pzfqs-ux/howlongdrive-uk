Driving Tips for This Route

For a comfortable drive on this UK route, plan your journey around peak traffic hours — typically 7:30–9:30 AM and 4:30–6:30 PM on weekdays. Motorway services such as Welcome Break, Moto, and Roadchef provide convenient stopping points for refuelling and refreshments. Remember that all speed limits are in miles per hour, and average speed cameras are increasingly common on major routes. At roundabouts, signal your intentions clearly and give way to the right. If driving in winter, watch for black ice on rural stretches and keep your headlights on during poor visibility conditions.

About HowLongDrive UK

HowLongDrive.uk is a free online tool that helps you quickly find driving times and distances between cities across the United Kingdom.

Whether you're planning a road trip, estimating commute times, or just curious how long it takes to drive somewhere, we've got you covered with accurate, easy-to-read journey information.

What We Offer

  • 7,600+ Route Pages - Pre-calculated driving times for thousands of UK city pairs
  • EV Trip Planning - Dedicated pages for electric vehicle journeys with charging considerations
  • City Hub Pages - See all routes from any major UK city at a glance
  • Road Trip Guides - Curated guides for popular UK driving routes
  • Mobile Friendly - Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops

Plan Your Journey

From quick trips like London to Brighton (1h 45m) to epic adventures like Land's End to John O'Groats (14+ hours), we help you plan with confidence.

How It Works

Simply search for your starting city and destination, or browse our routes by city. Each route page shows you:

  • Estimated driving time
  • Distance in miles
  • Route overview on an interactive map
  • Fuel cost estimates
  • Useful FAQs about the journey

About the Data

Our driving time estimates are based on typical road speeds for UK motorways, A-roads, and local roads. Actual journey times may vary depending on traffic, weather, roadworks, and your specific route.

We use mapping data to calculate realistic distances and times, but always allow extra time for your journey, especially during peak travel periods.
